# Feedback from Sohail Godall

**Date:** May 5, 2026
**Context:** Entrepreneur feedback on CashCast with ~30 users, no payments yet

---

## Key Insight

> "You don't need more users yet — you need better signal."

30 users with no payments means the value isn't landing strongly enough. It's not a traffic problem, it's a **positioning/validation problem**.

---

## Recommended Actions

### 1. Direct User Research
Speak to 20-30 people directly to understand:
- **Who** it's actually for
- **What** specific problem it solves
- **Whether** that problem is painful enough they'd pay for it

### 2. Tighten the Messaging
Within a few seconds, it should be obvious:
- What CashCast does
- Why someone should care

Current issue: messaging feels too broad, so people try it casually but don't stick or pay.

### 3. Validation Before Growth
> "Once even a few people say 'I'd pay for this', then you double down. Until then, growth tactics won't move the needle."

---

## Summary

| Problem | Current State | Target State |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Positioning | Too broad | Specific persona + pain point |
| Value prop | Unclear in seconds | Immediately obvious |
| Validation | 30 users, 0 payments | Users saying "I'd pay for this" |

---

## Action Items

- [ ] Conduct 20-30 user interviews
- [ ] Identify the most painful problem CashCast solves
- [ ] Rewrite landing page with tighter, specific messaging
- [ ] Find users willing to pay before scaling
