# Tally Form Setup Guide

**Time:** 3 minutes
**URL:** https://tally.so (sign up free, no credit card)

---

## Step 1: Create New Form

Click "Create form" → Start from scratch

---

## Step 2: Add Welcome Page

**Title:**
```
Help me build something useful for freelancers
```

**Description:**
```
I'm researching how freelancers manage irregular income. Takes 2 minutes. Your answers help me build a better tool.
```

**Button text:** Start

---

## Step 3: Add Questions (one per page)

### Question 1
- **Type:** Short text
- **Question:** What kind of freelance work do you do?
- **Placeholder:** e.g., UX design, web development, copywriting

### Question 2
- **Type:** Short text
- **Question:** How would you describe your income pattern?
- **Placeholder:** e.g., steady, irregular, feast-or-famine

### Question 3
- **Type:** Long text
- **Question:** Think about the last time you felt stressed about money. What was happening?
- **Placeholder:** Tell me the story...

### Question 4
- **Type:** Short text
- **Question:** How do you currently figure out if you can afford a big purchase?
- **Placeholder:** e.g., check bank app, spreadsheet, gut feeling

### Question 5
- **Type:** Long text
- **Question:** What's the most frustrating part of managing money with irregular income?

### Question 6
- **Type:** Long text
- **Question:** If a tool could show your bank balance 90 days from now, would that help? Why or why not?

### Question 7 (Optional)
- **Type:** Email
- **Question:** Want to chat for 15 min and get $20? Drop your email.
- **Required:** No
- **Placeholder:** your@email.com

---

## Step 4: Thank You Page

**Title:**
```
Thanks — this really helps.
```

**Description:**
```
I'll reach out if you left your email. Appreciate you taking the time.
```

---

## Step 5: Settings

1. Click the gear icon (Settings)
2. **Form name:** Freelancer Research
3. **Close form after X responses:** Leave off (or set to 50)
4. **Show Tally branding:** Your choice (free tier shows it)

---

## Step 6: Publish & Share

1. Click "Publish" (top right)
2. Copy the link
3. Share on Twitter, Reddit, Slack groups, etc.

---

## Pro Tips

- **Turn on email notifications** so you see responses immediately
- **Check "Insights"** tab in Tally to see response patterns
- **Export to CSV** when you have 20+ responses to analyze
