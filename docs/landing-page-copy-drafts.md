## Supporting Copy (Below the Fold)

### Problem Statement

> **Freelance income is unpredictable. Your bills aren't.**
>
> You know money is coming — you just don't know when. Meanwhile, rent, subscriptions, and quarterly taxes don't wait. One slow month and you're scrambling.

### How It Works (Simplified)

1. **Add your accounts** — Enter your current balances
2. **Add your bills & income** — One-time or recurring
3. **See your future** — 90-day forecast with your safe-to-spend amount

### One Differentiator (Not Three)

Current page leads with: forecasting + invoicing + time tracking + AI

**Recommended:** Lead with ONE. Save the rest for "More features" section.

> **The only cash flow app that connects invoicing to forecasting.**
>
> Send an invoice → it appears in your forecast. Client pays → your balance updates. No other app does this.

### Social Proof (When You Have It)

Current: "Join 50+ freelancers testing the beta"

**Better (when available):**
> "I finally stopped overdrafting." — Sarah, UX Designer
>
> "First time in 3 years I knew I could afford a vacation." — Marcus, Dev Consultant
