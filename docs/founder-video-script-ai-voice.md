# Founder Video Script - AI Voice Optimized

**Voice Settings (ElevenLabs):**
- Stability: 55%
- Clarity: 75%
- Style: 15%
- Speaker Boost: On

**Total Duration:** ~60 seconds

## Script with Pause Markers

```
I kept hearing the same thing from my freelancer friends...

"I have money in my account... but I don't know if I can actually spend it."

[2 second pause]

They'd get paid... feel good for a day...

Then remember rent is due in three weeks. And a client still hasn't paid.

Spreadsheets that took hours... and were wrong by Tuesday.

That constant, low-grade anxiety about money.

[1.5 second pause]

So I built Cashcast. For them.

It shows one number... what's actually safe to spend today. Without overdrafting later.

Add your bills. Your expected income. And see your real bank balance... ninety days into the future.

[1 second pause]

No bank connection required. No complicated setup.

Just clarity.

Can you afford that new laptop? Take that trip? Or do you need to chase that invoice first?

[1 second pause]

Try it free. Link's below.

[1.5 second pause]

I'm Omar. I built this for every freelancer... tired of guessing.
```

## Key Optimizations Made

| Original | Optimized | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| Long flowing sentences | Short punchy phrases | AI delivers short phrases more naturally |
| "90 days" | "ninety days" | Spelled-out numbers sound better |
| Continuous delivery | Strategic pauses | Gives weight to key moments |
| "link's below" | "Link's below" (separate sentence) | Clear CTA separation |
| Comma-separated lists | Period-separated | Forces natural pauses |

---

## Recommended Workflow

1. **Test with short segment first** - Generate just the hook (first 10 seconds) to test voice/settings
2. **Generate in sections** - If the full script sounds rushed, generate in 3-4 sections and combine
3. **Listen at 0.9x speed** - If it sounds good slowed down, your settings are right
4. **Export as WAV** - Better quality for video editing than MP3
