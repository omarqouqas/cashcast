# App Naming Strategy Session

**Date:** April 5, 2026
**Product:** Cash Flow Forecaster
**Current Domain:** cashflowforecaster.io / .app
**Goal:** Find a shorter, brandable name that works across iOS, Android, and web

---

## Product Summary

A cash flow forecasting PWA that projects bank balances 60 days into the future. Target audience: freelancers, gig workers, and anyone with irregular income who needs visibility into their financial runway.

**Key Value Props:**
- See your bank balance 60 days ahead
- Daily liquidity calendar
- "Can I afford it?" scenarios
- Monte Carlo probabilistic forecasting
- Invoice and income tracking for freelancers

---

## Naming Criteria (The Sacred Seven Lens)

When evaluating names, we consider:

1. **Memorability** - Can users recall it after hearing once?
2. **Spellability** - Easy to type without confusion?
3. **Domain Availability** - .io, .app, .com availability
4. **App Store Optimization** - Will it rank for relevant searches?
5. **Brand Extensibility** - Can you build a visual identity around it?
6. **Emotional Resonance** - Does it evoke the feeling you want?
7. **Global Readiness** - Works across languages/cultures?

## My Top 3 Picks (Ranked)

### 1. **Runway**
- **Why:** "Financial runway" is already a term people understand (especially freelancers and startup folks). It's short (6 letters), highly visual, and emotionally resonant. You can build a strong brand around it - imagine a logo with a horizon line or path.
- **Domain suggestions:** runway.money (ideal), tryrunway.io, runwayflow.io
- **App Store:** "Runway - Cash Flow Forecast" or "Runway - See Your Financial Future"

### 2. **Flowcast**
- **Why:** Perfect blend of your two core concepts (cash flow + forecast). Sounds modern and tech-forward without being cold. Easy to spell, easy to remember.
- **Domain suggestions:** flowcast.io (ideal), flowcast.app, getflowcast.com
- **App Store:** "Flowcast - 60-Day Cash Forecast"

### 3. **Sixty**
- **Why:** Unique, memorable, and directly references your 60-day forecasting window. Numeric names stand out in app stores. Short domain. Could expand tagline: "Sixty: See 60 Days Ahead"
- **Domain suggestions:** cashcast.money, getsixty.io, sixtycash.io
- **App Store:** "Sixty - 60-Day Cash Forecast"

For ASO (App Store Optimization), consider this format:


Examples:

This gives you brand identity + SEO keywords.

**Why Sixty Was Rejected (Valid Pushback):**

1. **Anchored to feature, not outcome** - Users don't care about the window length; they care about not getting blindsided
2. **Already factually wrong** - Product offers 90-day free / 365-day Pro, not 60 days
3. **Fails comprehension test** - "What does Sixty do?" → blank stares or "retirement app?"
4. **Not future-proof** - If you extend free tier, name becomes misleading
5. **Misses the emotional core** - The feeling is "safety/clarity," not "a number of days"

### Phase 7: SEO & Redirects (Day 5)

**CRITICAL for preserving SEO value**

#### 7a. Set Up 301 Redirects

In `next.config.js` or Vercel config:
```javascript
async redirects() {
  return [
    {
      source: '/:path*',
      has: [{ type: 'host', value: 'cashflowforecaster.io' }],
      destination: 'https://cashcast.money/:path*',
      permanent: true, // 301 redirect
    },
    {
      source: '/:path*',
      has: [{ type: 'host', value: 'www.cashflowforecaster.io' }],
      destination: 'https://cashcast.money/:path*',
      permanent: true,
    },
  ];
}
```

#### 7b. Google Search Console
1. Add `cashcast.money` as new property
2. Submit new sitemap: `https://cashcast.money/sitemap.xml`
3. Use "Change of Address" tool (if available)
4. Monitor indexing for 2-4 weeks
